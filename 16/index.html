Hi, I’m Jules. I’m a digital product designer that likes working with code.

Right now, I’m a staff product designer at Credit Karma, where I lead our design systems efforts. Before that, I did product design and web development work for Sequoia Capital, Chain, and Medium.

Outside of work, I’m building Women Who Design, a directory of accomplished women in the design industry. If you’d like to make a similar directory for underrepresented folks, you can fork the source code on GitHub. You can also support the continued development of the project by posting a job.
